How It Works

Bitey Zombies bite one random adjacent human per day. A successful bite (based on bite accuracy × zombie strength / 10) infects the human. Infected humans turn into zombies the following day.


Berserkers attempt to bite all adjacent humans in a single day. Each zombie gains +1 strength per day — at max strength, they die and return to human. Higher max strength = longer-lasting, more dangerous zombies.
